About Wentworth Miller

Wentworth Miller was born on June 2,1972.He was born in Chipping Norton, Oxfordshire, England.He is an actor

,screenwriter and producer.He is mostly known from his role in Prison Break as Michael Scofield

His character, a brilliant engineer who orchestrates the escape from a maximum-security prison

to save his brother, made him a household name.
